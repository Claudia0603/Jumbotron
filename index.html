Soccer

I have played soccer since I was about 3 years old. I played in JAWS till I was 7 then I joined the Wolves. When my baby brother was born I left the wolves because it was a travel league. I then played for my middle school. I went to tryouts my freshman year and then didn't make the team, but this year I tried again and made it. I can't really to you why I like soccer but when I play it just makes me happy even if I lose a game or two. Plus the friends I have from playing soccer are the best.

Volunteering at my Church

I volunteer at my Church's nursery and take care of little kids while there parents go to "Big Church". I do it for Community Service but I love it. I normally watch 3 to 4 year olds, but if another age group needs me I help them. I read books to them, play cars, kitchen, dinosaurs, whatever they want to do. I have always loved little kids and volunteering in the nursery allows me to have fun with them and get my service hours.
